"Freedom in Fishbowl"

  • USER TOOLS :  PENCIL, 0.1 PEN, 0.05 PEN, 0.3 PEN, PAPER
  • PLACE : Hanwon museum of art
  • SIZE : 788X1090mm



I saw National Geographic, and I found a humpback whale. I fell in love with the mystery of the humpback whales, so I chose humpback whales and oceans as the theme and made it my last senior year's work.

When I look at humpback whales in the ocean, I want to express that graceful with my pen. Before I finished,

I drew several humpback whales, and the last picture was taken. (788X1090mm) And I was lucky to have two customers who wanted to buy my work, so I sold it to one person and finished the exhibition.
